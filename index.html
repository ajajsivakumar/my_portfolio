About Me

I’m Ajay Siva Kumar, a dedicated 3D animator with hands-on experience working on real-time animation projects for the past 2+ years. My journey began with a strong passion for storytelling through animation, and over time, I’ve worked on diverse professional projects that helped me grow as an artist and animator.

Throughout my career, I’ve worked on Real-time Projects across different areas like Kid’s Animation, Gaming character loops,and Movie previsualization.In kid’s animations, I focused on expressive, fun character performances that appeal to young audiences. In Gaming projects,I created smooth animation loops such as idle, walk, and action cycles optimized for real-time engines.My involvement in Movie previsualization helped me understand cinematic storytelling, as I worked on camera blocking, staging, and action choreography.I also enjoy developing original creative shots where I experiment with acting and movement to bring out emotion and personality in characters.

I mainly work with Autodesk Maya for character animation, scene layout, and precise control over timing and acting. For stylized and experimental projects, I use Blender, especially for quick previews, creative tests, and rendering. Working with both tools gives me the flexibility to adapt to different pipelines and project requirements, whether it's game-ready loops, cinematic sequences, or stylized animations.

I’m currently expanding my skillset further and i’m open to exciting collaborations that challenge and inspire me.
